Three problems. One system.
Revenue Leakage
Missed appointments. Calls not returned. Slots that should fill, don't. Every unanswered inquiry is a patient walking to the next clinic.
A clinic missing just 10 calls a day at ₹1,000 per consultation loses ₹3 lakh a month. That is ₹36 lakh a year, without realising it.
Source: Doxmate, Dec 2025
60% of patients abandon a call if they wait longer than one minute.
Source: Hellomatik, 2025
Broken Systems
Your HMS handles records. Your receptionist handles calls. Your WhatsApp handles bookings. None of them talk to each other. The gaps between your systems cost you more than the systems themselves.
Clinic staff spend an average of 11 hours per week on repetitive admin. Manual confirmations alone: 4 hours. 67% of Indian clinics under 10 doctors still manage this manually.
Source: WebMarv audit, 15+ Indian clinics, May 2026
Fragmented Care
A patient sees 4 doctors across your clinic and 3 outside it. No single doctor has the full picture. Decisions get made on incomplete information. Not because doctors are careless. Because the system never connected the pieces.
Patients visit multiple facilities, public and private, leading to fragmentation of medical information across providers.
Source: PMC Kerala study on patient-held records in Indian healthcare
